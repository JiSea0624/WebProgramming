Our Social Responsibility

At Fioreca, we believe that businesses have a role to play in creating a positive impact on the world. Our commitment to social responsibility is at the heart of everything we do, and we strive to ensure our business practices align with our values of sustainability, community support, and ethical craftsmanship.


1. Sustainable Materials

Fioreca is committed to minimizing our environmental impact by using high-quality, eco-friendly materials wherever possible. Our pipe cleaners and other crafting supplies are sourced from trusted suppliers who share our commitment to reducing waste and promoting sustainable practices.


2. Supporting Local Artisans

We take pride in being a small business that celebrates creativity and craftsmanship. By purchasing from Fioreca, you’re supporting local artisans who pour their passion and skills into every handcrafted bouquet. Your support helps us sustain jobs and contribute to the local economy.


3. Promoting Creativity and Well-Being

We encourage creativity as a form of self-expression and well-being. Fioreca regularly donates crafting kits and supplies to local schools, community centers, and charities to inspire creativity among children and adults alike. We believe that art has the power to bring people together and enhance mental health.


4. Giving Back to the Community

Fioreca proudly partners with local organizations and charities to support causes that matter. A portion of our proceeds is donated to initiatives that promote environmental conservation, mental health awareness, and community development. With every bouquet you purchase, you’re contributing to these meaningful efforts.

5. Advocacy for Handmade Craftsmanship

In a world of mass production, Fioreca champions the beauty of handmade creations. By choosing handcrafted bouquets, you’re not only celebrating individuality and artistry but also helping us reduce the demand for factory-produced goods that often contribute to environmental harm.


We’re dedicated to making a difference, one handcrafted bouquet at a time. Thank you for choosing Fioreca and joining us in our journey toward a more beautiful and responsible world!
