Accessibility Statement

At Fioreca, we are committed to ensuring that our website is accessible to everyone, including individuals with disabilities. We strive to provide a seamless experience for all users, regardless of their abilities, by following best practices and accessibility standards.


Our Accessibility Goals

We aim to:

  • Make our website navigable and usable for all customers.
  • Provide equal access to all our services, including browsing and purchasing our handcrafted pipe cleaner flower bouquets.

Accessibility Features

Our website includes the following accessibility features:

  • Keyboard Navigation: Our website is fully navigable using a keyboard.
  • Text Alternatives: Images, including product photos, have descriptive alt text for screen readers.
  • Readable Text: We use high-contrast text and easy-to-read fonts to enhance visibility.
  • Responsive Design: Our website is designed to be functional on various devices, including desktops, tablets, and mobile phones.
  • Accessible Forms: All forms include labels, instructions, and error notifications that are accessible to assistive technologies.

Ongoing Improvements

We recognize that accessibility is an ongoing effort. We continuously evaluate our website and make enhancements to ensure that it meets the needs of our diverse customer base.
