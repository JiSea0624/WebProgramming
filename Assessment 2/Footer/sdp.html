Shipping and Delivery Policy

Welcome to Fioreca! We’re committed to ensuring your handcrafted pipe cleaner flower bouquets are delivered to you with care and in a timely manner. Please review our shipping and delivery policy below.


Processing Time

  • All orders are handcrafted with care and typically require 3-5 business days for processing before they are shipped.
  • Custom orders or large quantities may require additional time. We will notify you of any delays via email.

Shipping Options and Costs

  • Standard Shipping (3-5 days): 15 aed.
  • Express Shipping (1-2 days): 25 aed.
  • Same-Day Shipping (1 & 3 pieces only): 10 aed.
  • Free Shipping (orders over 250aed): Free.
  • Pick Up/Meet Up: Free.

Delivery Times

Delivery times depend on your chosen shipping method and location:

  • Domestic Orders: Typically arrive within 3-5 business days after shipping.

Order Tracking

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can use this number to monitor your shipment’s progress.


Packaging

All bouquets are carefully packaged to protect their structure and beauty during transit. We strive to use eco-friendly materials wherever possible.


Shipping Restrictions

  • At this time, we cannot ship to P.O. boxes or APO/FPO addresses.
  • Some international destinations may have restrictions due to customs regulations. Customers are responsible for understanding the regulations in their destination country.

Lost or Delayed Shipments

  • Fioreca is not responsible for delays caused by shipping carriers or customs.
  • If your order is significantly delayed, please contact us at fiorecabloom@gmail.com with your order details.

Damaged or Incorrect Items

If your order arrives damaged or if you receive the wrong item, please email us at fiorecabloom@gmail.com within 48 hours of delivery. Include photos of the item and packaging to help us resolve the issue promptly.
