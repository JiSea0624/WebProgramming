About Us

Why Choose Us?

At Fioreca, we capture the essence of beauty in its simplest form with our eco-friendly, handcrafted pipe cleaner flowers. Timeless and low-maintenance, they make perfect gifts, unique decorations, or custom arrangements tailored to your style.

Each piece is crafted with care and passion, bringing enduring beauty to brighten any space or occasion.

History

Brand Name Definition

  • The name "Fioreca" is inspired by the Italian word fiore, meaning "flower."

Actual History

  • Fioreca is a small business launched on March 29, 2025, with a mission to create handmade flowers that never wither or break easily, ensuring they remain beautiful for months after purchase.
  • The business was founded by its owner at just 19 years old, while she was still in college. Determined to find a way to earn money and preserve the beauty of flowers, she stumbled upon inspiration while scrolling through TikTok. With a spark of creativity and months of hard work, she turned her idea into reality, officially launching Fioreca.
